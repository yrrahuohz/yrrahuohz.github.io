AI Education

Nowadays, as the AI is keep developing, AI can now be use as a way of education! There is mainly two ways of AI education:AI teaching and online class or meeting.

AI teaching

AI is now commonly use to educate children and to help teenagers' learning, and it shows a great advantage, being more accurate, and can be used in any time. For instance, online website such as IXL, readwork, and APP like Khan academy, etc.

Online meeting

Presently, effected by the covid-19, more and more teachers start practicing online learning, and seeing the benifit of it, more school plan to create online teaching class, which you learn in your home. Its benifit is people can learn thing easily although they are far away from school. The online meeting APPs are for example, Teams, Zoom, Tencent meeting, etc.
